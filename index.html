Healing Through Movement

Welcome to my practice in Utrecht. I'm Dr. Liana, a clinical psychologist specializing in dance therapy. Together, we'll explore the transformative power of movement and psychology.

About Dr. Liana

As a clinical psychologist with a specialization in dance therapy, I believe in the profound connection between mind, body, and spirit. Based in the beautiful city of Utrecht, I've dedicated my career to helping individuals discover healing through the art of movement.

My approach combines evidence-based psychological practices with the expressive freedom of dance therapy, creating a unique therapeutic experience that addresses both mental and physical well-being.

Qualifications & Experience

  • Licensed Clinical Psychologist
  • Certified Dance Movement Therapist
  • 10+ years of clinical experience
  • Specialized in trauma and anxiety disorders
