Personal Statement

As a dedicated clinical psychologist and certified dance movement therapist, I am passionate about integrating evidence-based psychological practices with the transformative power of movement. My approach emphasizes the profound connection between mind, body, and spirit, creating therapeutic experiences that address the whole person.

With over a decade of clinical experience, I specialize in trauma recovery, anxiety disorders, and helping individuals discover their authentic selves through embodied healing practices. I believe that every individual has the innate capacity for healing and growth, and my role is to provide a safe, supportive space for this journey.
